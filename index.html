About Me

We love plants

An experienced, published scientist, Dmitri brings an unparalleled level of customer service and attention to detail in all his projects. Dmitri managed over one hundred small, medium, and large-scale developments and habitat restoration programs at a local, state-wide, and national level. Major projects include ecological planning for linear infrastructure and major subdivisions. Medium projects include soil and groundwater investigations for airport civil works. Minor projects include habitat restoration plans, acid sulphate soil investigations, and koala habitat assessments for local developments. In 2010, Dmitri began a career in environmental science working as a researcher for the Sierra Nevada Research Institute at the University of California, Merced. In 2015 he began a career as an environmental scientist based in Tweed Heads, Australia.
